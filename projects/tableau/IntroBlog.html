The Changing Global Diet: Intro Blog

We are biodiversity scientists who went looking to find which foods, if any, have disappeared from human diets across the globe.

We dug into food supply data collected from FAOStat from 152 countries across 50 years (1961-2009).

We didn't find any foods that have been lost, but we did find massive change.

Here's five surprising ways people's diets have changed over the past 50 years

Investigate how global food supplies have changed for 152 countries over 50 years with interactive visuals on our new website, The Changing Global Diet.
